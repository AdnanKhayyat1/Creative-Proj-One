How we asses our reviews:


pH Value

For us humans, a perfect pH value would be more alkaline (around 8~9). This helps us fight Bacteria and Microbes.


Price

The cheaper the water bottle, the better. However, a water bottle too cheap could potentially raise a red flag.


Personal Taste

I sample each bottled water and criticize the taste. My criticism hinges on four parameters:

  1. Taste of Sweetness or Sourness in the water
  2. Taste of minerals
  3. Whether the water feels 'bouyant' in your mouth, and other textural remarks
  4. Whether there is an aftertaste or not

Recycability
